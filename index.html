Our Solution: The 2-Session System

We treat our discussion as a high-value product. To create a great product, you need a rock-solid process. We separate Preparation from Performance.

Session 1

The "Inquiry"

This session guarantees everyone is prepared.

  • Format: We read the paper *together* in a collaborative session.
  • Tactic: We collectively generate a list of 5-10 high-quality questions.
  • Outcome: A shared "agenda" for Session 2. No more blank stares.
Session 2

The "Breakdown"

This is our high-value "performance" session.

  • Presentation (20m): One member presents a concise summary (rotates weekly).
  • Discussion (40m): We use our question list to run a deep, structured, and critical discussion.
  • Outcome: A meeting you can be proud to invite faculty and advisors to.
